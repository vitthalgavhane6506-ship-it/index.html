About Me

The Story Behind
The Edit

Hi, I'm Vitthal Gavhane — a Senior Video Editor who believes that great editing is invisible. The cut you don't notice, the rhythm that keeps you watching, the moment that hits harder than you expected.

With over 2.5 years of hands-on experience across podcasts, documentary-style content, reels, food vlogs, advertisements, and digital storytelling, I've had the privilege of shaping content that has reached millions of viewers across platforms.

My greatest strength is understanding content deeply — identifying the emotional core of raw footage and transforming it into a visual narrative that connects. From growing Vaicharik Kida from 691K to 978K+ subscribers, to crafting high-impact teasers and promotional films, my focus has always been on one thing: keeping the audience watching.

I don't just edit clips together. I tell stories.
